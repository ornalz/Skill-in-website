WHY SKILL-IN

5 things Skill-in delivers.

01 · Career Bridge
Closing the gap to the job market

The platform builds a Performance-based Portfolio for every student — concrete, evidence-driven proof of skills that creates a real competitive edge with potential employers.

02 · PBL
Solving real-world needs

The system connects students to tangible technological and social challenges from the field. Working in cross-functional Squads on real organizational problems drives high engagement and meaningful learning through hands-on practice.

03 · Student-Facing AI
A Ready-to-code workspace

A technology layer built around the most widely used industry tools (GitHub, Trello, Figma). Using AI, the platform spins up a Ready-to-code environment for students so the squad can focus on execution and problem-solving from day one.

04 · Faculty Control
Pedagogical control & faculty dashboard

Faculty get a centralized dashboard to manage dozens of projects in parallel in real time. The system embeds the syllabus and course content directly into the students' execution Backlog and provides an objective view of team progress, work quality and pedagogical outcomes.

05 · Objective Data
Data-driven performance evaluation

Skill-in provides a deep, objective analysis of every student based on their actual activity within the platform. The AI surfaces data-driven insights covering both technical capabilities and soft skills — teamwork, initiative and problem-solving — enabling high-quality tracking of individual and team progress.

THE PLATFORM

A platform for building
real tech-industry experience.

01 · Project selection
Participants pick a challenge and a squad

A catalog of real social needs aligned to the UN SDG goals — students apply for a role on the squad and join a cross-functional team.

02 · The squad room
A complete workspace for every Squad

The Squad Room is a single space where the team gets the brief, full integration with industry tools (GitHub, Figma, Trello), sprint management, real-time performance data and a built-in AI mentor that guides them toward the solution.

03 · Faculty control
Pedagogical control & faculty dashboard

Faculty get a centralized dashboard to manage dozens of projects in parallel in real time — entering squad rooms to provide help, scheduling meetings, tracking progress and identifying teams that need intervention. An objective view of execution quality and pedagogical outcomes.

04 · Task builder
Academic content inside the practice

A pedagogical management interface that lets faculty embed academic content directly into the live project. With a smart AI assistant, the system translates course requirements into well-defined tasks (Backlog), splits them by role and sprint, and gives students a clear execution roadmap.

05 · Data-driven evaluation
Custom Metrics for success

An advanced interface that lets faculty and employers define the exact evaluation criteria. The system analyzes performance data in real time and surfaces insights on development quality, analytical ability, team communication and soft skills — all weighted to match the goals predefined in each student's Portfolio.
